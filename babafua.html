About Us

Your Trusted Laundry Partner Since 2010

At Babafua Laundry, we believe in providing exceptional laundry services that make your life easier.

Founded in 2010, we have grown from a small local laundromat to a full-service laundry provider with state-of-the-art equipment and eco-friendly practices. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to delivering the highest quality service with a quick turnaround time.

We understand that your clothes are important to you, which is why we treat each item with the utmost care and attention to detail. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for laundry services in the area.
